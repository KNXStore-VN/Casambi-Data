- DALI Broadcast (RGB, TW, 2-4 channels)
- DALI DT8 Tunable White (2700-6500K, 3000-5000K)

**DALI Standalone:** DALI driver connects directly (no external DALI supply)  
**0-10V:** Isolated output to 0-10V dimmer/driver  
**Relay:** SSR output only for external relay, NOT driver mains input
